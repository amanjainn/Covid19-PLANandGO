In the fight to quell the spread of COVID-19, containment is currently the best weapon the globe has in its arsenal.The idea of Plan&Go is to maintain a digital queue instead of crowding near any store/hospital. Users can book a time slot and visit the store at the allotted time to avoid queues and hence maintain Social Distancing.

Covid making a living off the crowds

Coronavirus is deadly, we already know that from the staggering death toll from across the globe. But, what is even more alarming is the speed at which it spreads. Social distancing counters the spread of the deadly virus prevening sick people from coming in close contact with healthy people in order to reduce opportunities for disease transmission.Therefore it is important to follow the guidelines to save yourself and your community from getting infected

Departmental Store

Many people come to departmental store for buying basic necessities, which thus leads to formation of crowds which is very dangerous as it might aide in the spread of Corona virus.

Bank Queue

Many people come to Bank for withdrawing money in these tough times, which leads to formation of groups in large numbers which is not suitable keeping in mind the conditions we have right now.

Jewelry Shops

As demands of Jewelry plunged due to this covid-19 virus, so did the prices, so people are forming up on the jewelry shops to buy jewelery at low prices, thus forming groups and violating social distancing norms.

Supermarket

As some basic items might not be available at the departmental stores, people form up on supermarkets to buy items and in bulk, which is against social distancing norms and also, people should not buy in bulk as stocks of those products are limited.

Bakery

As people are fed up eating the same food again and again at their homes, they line up in a bakery for having a delicious bite of goods, not following the social distancing norms, thus leading to formation of groups which is highly not advisable right now.

Liquor shops

As people who have a habit of drinking regularly and even those who don't, want to have a good time in these hard times as it is very stressful thus grouping up on liquor shops for buying alcohol, which is very risky in these times.

How we solve the problem?

STEP 1

See a crowd at a store? Sign up as a user or owner of the shop.Then sign in.

STEP 2

Search for your desired store.

STEP 3

Click on book slot to book a time slot to visit the shop.This will help in avoiding mass gathering and only limited people can visit a shop at a given time.

STEP 4

Confirm your slot and a mail will be sent with the details.

FINISH

Visit the store at the allotted time. Maintain social distancing and save lives!
