In the fight to quell the spread of COVID-19, containment is currently the best weapon the globe has in its arsenal.The idea of Plan&Go is to maintain a digital queue instead of crowding near any store/hospital. Users can book a time slot and visit the store at the allotted time to avoid queues and hence maintain Social Distancing.
